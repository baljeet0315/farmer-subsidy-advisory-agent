SCHEME: Krishak Unnati Yojana (Chhattisgarh)
Level: state
Source: https://aimindia.in/chhattisgarh-krishak-unnati-scheme/ (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Chhattisgarh input-subsidy and paddy support: paddy procured at Rs 3,100 per quintal, up to 21 quintal per acre, with the differential ('antar') amount paid directly to registered farmers' bank accounts. Supports input costs for seeds, fertiliser, and machinery.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Replaced the discontinued Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana. Requires registration as a paddy-selling farmer in Chhattisgarh.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Chhattisgarh land records (B-1/khasra)
- Bank account
- Registration at the paddy procurement (dhan kharidi) centre
- Girdawari entry

HOW TO APPLY
Register at the village paddy procurement society / cooperative before the Kharif marketing season; benefit is paid directly after procurement.

WHERE TO APPLY
Village Primary Agricultural Cooperative Society (PACS) / dhan kharidi kendra
