SCHEME: Crop Residue Management (CRM) Machinery Subsidy (Punjab)
Level: state
Source: https://agrimachinerypb.com (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
50% subsidy for individual farmers (80% for Custom Hiring Centres / FPOs) on crop-residue machinery — Happy Seeder, Super Seeder, Smart Seeder, Mulcher, Baler, Super SMS — to stop paddy-stubble burning and sow wheat into standing residue.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
For Punjab landowning farmers managing paddy residue; targets the stubble-burning problem. CHCs/FPOs get a higher 80% subsidy.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Land records (jamabandi)
- Bank account
- Tractor RC (for mounted machines)
- Recent photograph

HOW TO APPLY
Apply online at agrimachinerypb.com within the notified window; department verifies and issues a permission letter before purchase.

WHERE TO APPLY
agrimachinerypb.com / Block Agriculture Officer
