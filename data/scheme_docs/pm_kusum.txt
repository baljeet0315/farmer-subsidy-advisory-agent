SCHEME: PM-KUSUM (Solar Pump Scheme) - Component B
Level: national
Source: https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/pm-kusum (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Subsidy for installing a stand-alone solar agriculture pump (up to 7.5 HP) for off-grid farms. Central + state subsidy covers up to 60% of cost, ~30% bank loan, remainder by the farmer. Reduces diesel dependence and irrigation cost.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Component B targets individual farmers in off-grid / low-grid areas. Component C (grid-connected pump solarisation) is a separate track.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Land records
- Bank account
- Photograph
- Authorisation/declaration of irrigation need

HOW TO APPLY
Apply through the state nodal agency portal or the national PM-KUSUM portal; the discom/agency verifies and allots a vendor.

WHERE TO APPLY
pmkusum.mnre.gov.in / State renewable energy / agriculture department
