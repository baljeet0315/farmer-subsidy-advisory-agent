SCHEME: Crop Diversification Programme - Kharif Maize Incentive (Punjab)
Level: state
Source: https://pdc.punjab.gov.in/en/pdc-key-sectors/agriculture-page/crop-diversification/ (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Cash incentive of Rs 17,500 per hectare for paddy farmers who shift to Kharif maize, to save groundwater and diversify away from water-intensive rice. Expanded to 16 districts for 2026-27.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
For Punjab paddy growers (owners) willing to switch to maize. Districts include Amritsar, Gurdaspur, Hoshiarpur, Ludhiana, Patiala, Sangrur and others.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Land records
- Bank account
- Proof of paddy cultivation (girdawari)
- Undertaking to sow maize

HOW TO APPLY
Register with the district Agriculture Department under the Crop Diversification Programme before the Kharif season; incentive is paid on verified maize sowing.

WHERE TO APPLY
District Agriculture Department (Punjab) / Chief Agriculture Officer
