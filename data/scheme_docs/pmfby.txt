SCHEME: PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana) - Crop Insurance
Level: national
Source: https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/pmfby (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Crop insurance against natural calamities, pests and disease. Farmer pays only 2% premium for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, and 5% for commercial/horticultural crops; the rest is government-subsidised. From Kharif 2026, wild-animal attack and paddy inundation are added as localised add-on covers.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Only notified crops in notified areas are covered. Paddy is a notified crop in Chhattisgarh.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Land records or tenancy/sowing certificate
- Bank account
- Sowing declaration
- Khasra/khatauni

HOW TO APPLY
Loanee farmers are enrolled automatically through their financing bank; non-loanee farmers enrol voluntarily before the cut-off date via the PMFBY portal, bank, CSC, or insurance agent.

WHERE TO APPLY
pmfby.gov.in / Financing bank / CSC
