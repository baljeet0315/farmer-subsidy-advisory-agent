SCHEME: Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
Level: national
Source: https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/kcc (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Short-term crop loan at 7% interest, effectively as low as 4% with the prompt-repayment interest subvention. Collateral-free up to Rs 2 lakh. Five-year validity with flexible withdrawal.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Owner cultivators, tenant farmers, oral lessees, sharecroppers, and SHGs/JLGs are all eligible. Most useful for farmers who do not already hold a KCC.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Application form
- Aadhaar/Voter ID/Driving Licence
- Address proof
- Land holding proof certified by revenue authority
- Two passport photos
- Cropping pattern with acreage

HOW TO APPLY
Apply at any bank branch or online via the Kisan Rin Portal / Jan Samarth Portal. Security documents needed only above Rs 1.60 lakh.

WHERE TO APPLY
Any commercial/cooperative/RRB bank / Kisan Rin Portal
