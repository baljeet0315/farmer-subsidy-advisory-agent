SCHEME: PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
Level: national
Source: https://www.myscheme.gov.in/schemes/pm-kisan (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Income support of Rs 6,000 per year paid in three equal installments of Rs 2,000 directly to the bank account of eligible landholding farmer families.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Excludes income-tax payers, government employees, pensioners drawing >Rs 10,000/month, and institutional landholders. These exclusions are advisory (not captured in the profile).

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Land ownership records (khasra/khatauni)
- Bank account linked to Aadhaar
- Mobile number

HOW TO APPLY
Register online at pmkisan.gov.in (Farmer's Corner > New Farmer Registration) or via the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC), then complete e-KYC.

WHERE TO APPLY
pmkisan.gov.in / Common Service Centre (CSC) / Village revenue officer
