SCHEME: Mukhyamantri Vriksha Sampada Yojana (Chhattisgarh)
Level: state
Source: https://aimindia.in/mukhyamantri-vriksha-sampada-yojana/ (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Promotes commercial tree plantation on private land. 100% financial subsidy on inputs for plantations up to 5 acres and 50% beyond 5 acres, with a minimum planting density of 160 trees per acre. Raises long-term farmer income from high-value timber.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Applicant must be a Chhattisgarh resident holding valid land title. Good fit for farmers with fallow/under-used land.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Chhattisgarh land title / legal land-use document
- Bank account
- Plantation plan (min 160 trees/acre)

HOW TO APPLY
Apply online via the scheme portal / forest-agriculture department with land documents and a planting plan; field verification follows.

WHERE TO APPLY
Chhattisgarh Forest / Agriculture Department portal
