SCHEME: Crop Loss Relief under SDRF/NDRF (Punjab)
Level: state
Source: https://agri.punjab.gov.in/ (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Because Punjab does not implement PMFBY crop insurance, crop-loss compensation is provided under SDRF/NDRF norms — up to about Rs 17,000 per hectare for losses (subject to notified disaster and area limits). This is the state's de-facto insurance substitute.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Punjab has NOT joined PMFBY (opted out; still out as of 2026), so farmers rely on SDRF/NDRF relief for crop loss. Compensation is disaster-triggered, not a premium-based insurance.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Land records
- Bank account
- Girdawari / crop-loss assessment (special girdawari) by revenue staff

HOW TO APPLY
After a notified natural calamity, the revenue department conducts a special girdawari; eligible farmers receive compensation directly. No prior enrolment needed.

WHERE TO APPLY
District Revenue / Deputy Commissioner office (special girdawari)
