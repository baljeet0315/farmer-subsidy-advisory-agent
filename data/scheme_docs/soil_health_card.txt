SCHEME: Soil Health Card Scheme
Level: national
Source: https://soilhealth.dac.gov.in (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Free soil testing and a Soil Health Card reporting fertility and nutrient status with crop-wise fertiliser recommendations. Helps cut fertiliser cost by 20-30% and raise yields by 10-15%. Entirely free of cost.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Open to all landholding farmers; small and marginal farmers are prioritised.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Land records (jamabandi)
- Mobile number
- PM-KISAN ID (optional)

HOW TO APPLY
Apply online at soilhealth.dac.gov.in or the SHC app (Farmer's Corner), or offline at a local agriculture office / soil-testing lab / CSC; card is issued in 15-30 days after sample collection.

WHERE TO APPLY
soilhealth.dac.gov.in / District agriculture office / CSC
