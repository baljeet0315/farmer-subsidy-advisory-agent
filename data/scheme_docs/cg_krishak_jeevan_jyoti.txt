SCHEME: Krishak Jeevan Jyoti Yojana (Chhattisgarh)
Level: state
Source: https://aimindia.in/chhattisgarh-krishak-jeevan-jyoti-yojana/ (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Free / highly subsidised electricity for agricultural irrigation pumps in Chhattisgarh: up to 6,000 units per year free for pumps up to 3 HP, or a flat Rs 100 per HP plan. Lowers irrigation power cost substantially.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
For Chhattisgarh farmers with an agricultural pump electricity connection (irrigated farms).

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Electricity consumer code/number
- Latest farmland papers
- Caste certificate (if applicable)
- Bank account

HOW TO APPLY
Submit the subsidy form with consumer code at the local electricity sub-station or CSPDCL office.

WHERE TO APPLY
CSPDCL (Chhattisgarh State Power Distribution Co.) sub-station / office
