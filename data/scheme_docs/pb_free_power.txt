SCHEME: Free Agricultural Power Supply (Punjab / PSPCL)
Level: state
Source: https://www.pspcl.in (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Punjab provides zero-cost electricity to agricultural (AP-category) tubewell/pump connections — the only state in India with a full waiver of farm power charges. Covers 14+ lakh agricultural pump connections.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
For Punjab farmers who own land with an agricultural tubewell/pump (irrigated) electricity connection.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Agricultural electricity connection (AP) consumer number
- Land records
- Bank account

HOW TO APPLY
Farmers with an agricultural pump connection are billed at zero; new connections are applied for at the local PSPCL office with land and identity documents.

WHERE TO APPLY
PSPCL (Punjab State Power Corporation Ltd) sub-division office
