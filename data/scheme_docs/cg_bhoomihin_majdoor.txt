SCHEME: Deendayal Upadhyay Bhoomihin Krishi Majdoor Kalyan Yojana (Chhattisgarh)
Level: state
Source: https://www.govtschemes.in/chhattisgarh-deendayal-upadhyay-bhoomihin-krishi-majdoor-kalyan-yojana (verified 2026-06-29)

OVERVIEW
Annual financial assistance of Rs 10,000 to landless agricultural labourer families in Chhattisgarh, paid directly to the Aadhaar-linked bank account. Also covers traditional village-service (Pauni-Pasari) workers and certain tribal priests.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE
Specifically for landless workers (no agricultural land). This is the key scheme for farmers/labourers who own no land.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED
- Aadhaar card
- Proof of Chhattisgarh residence
- Aadhaar-linked bank account (for e-KYC)
- Declaration of landless agricultural-labour livelihood

HOW TO APPLY
Apply through the gram panchayat / urban body; benefit is credited directly in installments after verification.

WHERE TO APPLY
Gram Panchayat / Janpad / Urban local body
